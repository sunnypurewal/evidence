# Code of Conduct

`evidence` aims to be a practical, respectful open-source project.

Be direct, kind, and specific. Discuss technical tradeoffs on their merits. Do not harass, threaten, demean, or exclude people based on identity, background, experience level, or viewpoint.

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that are abusive, spammy, off-topic, or unsafe for a public repository. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from participating.

If you need to report a conduct concern, open a private channel with the maintainers rather than posting sensitive details publicly.
